How to Find SEO Topics in Forums Based on Your Niche

Forums are one of the most underrated sources for SEO content ideas. They reveal real questions, real problems, and the exact language your audience uses.

By exploring niche-specific communities, you can uncover long-tail keywords that are often ignored by traditional keyword tools.

Look for Repeated Questions

The best opportunities come from recurring discussions. If multiple users are asking the same thing, that is a strong signal of demand.

Turning these discussions into structured blog posts gives you a natural advantage in ranking, since your content directly matches user intent.
